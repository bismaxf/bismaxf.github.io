Garlic Seasame Noodles

Why did the garlic sesame noodles go to therapy? They had too much emotional baggage-laden with soy-cial anxiety. So here is an easy and go-to recipe of seasame noodles.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g (about 9 oz) of your favorite noodles
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Noodles: Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauce Preparation:In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, and vinegar. Set aside.
  3. Cooking:Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Combine Sauce and Noodles:Pour the prepared sauce over the sautéed garlic. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss until well coated in the sauce.
  5. Serve:Transfer the sesame garlic noodles to a serving dish. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  6. Enjoy:Serve the sesame garlic noodles warm, and enjoy your delicious and flavorful dish
